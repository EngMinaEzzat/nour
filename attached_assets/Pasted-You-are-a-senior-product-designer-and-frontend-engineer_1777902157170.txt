You are a senior product designer and frontend engineer. I want you to completely redesign and implement the storefront/homepage of my girls fashion and cosmetics ecommerce web app into a modern, premium, professional, conversion-focused web experience.

Project context:
- The app sells fashion and cosmetics for girls/women.
- Target audience: stylish young women and girls who care about fashion, makeup, skincare, self-care, trends, cute aesthetics, and easy shopping.
- The new design should feel premium, feminine, modern, clean, fast, trustworthy, and highly visual.

Main goal:
Transform the storefront into a premium fashion + cosmetics landing page that makes the brand feel professional and trustworthy, increases browsing, and encourages users to shop.

Design direction:
- Use a modern editorial look with a feminine luxury feel.
- Color palette: soft blush pink, warm ivory, champagne beige, clean white, deep charcoal/black for contrast, and optional rose-gold accents.
- Typography: elegant high-impact serif or display font for headings, clean modern sans-serif for body and UI.
- Use large visual sections, generous spacing, rounded cards where appropriate, subtle shadows, smooth hover states, and cinematic transitions.
- The design should look beautiful on desktop and mobile.
- Prioritize mobile responsiveness because many shoppers will browse from phones.
