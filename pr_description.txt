🎨 Palette: Add Boho-Chic visual theme and presets

💡 What:
Introduces a brand-new "boho-chic" style and "boho" personality for the fashion and cosmetics storefront. This includes extending the TypeScript unions in `store-config.ts`, updating English and Arabic localized strings with a relaxed, desert-inspired copy under `defaultSections`, and modifying the section factory to dynamically use these assets when the user selects the "boho-chic" theme. Missing common button ARIA labels (`next`, `previous`, `remove`) were also added to the translations.

🎯 Why:
To offer a warm, free-spirited, and relaxed visual alternative for merchants who want a nature-inspired and bohemian aesthetic without replacing or deleting the existing "elegant" default style presets.

📸 Before/After:
Before: The section factory strictly mapped string defaults that could only change between fashion and cosmetics categories.
After: The section factory uses a conditionally passed `style` parameter to map localized copy and specific fallback images (like `/hero-boho-optimized.jpg`) for the "boho-chic" theme.

♿ Accessibility:
Ensured the newly mapped icon buttons and text strings utilize proper `t()` variables, and included standard `common.buttons` localizations across English and Arabic to support `aria-label` translations robustly.
